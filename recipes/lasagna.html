Lasagna Recipe

Home

This simple lasagna dish only takes 10 minutes to make, and can feed up to 8.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian season, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles, homemade or store-bought
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
